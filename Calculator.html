GPA Calculator

How to use:

Input your data below. The name/code of the course is optional, but recommended.
Enter the credit weight of that course (3 credits for most one-semester courses).
Enter your letter grade (F-A+. You can also enter P, but this does not affect your GPA).
Tick the first box if the course is within your major, i.e. EECS courses for Computer Science majors. This will count that course in your "Major GPA".
Tick the second box if you would like the course to count towards a custom GPA, e.g. to calculate EECS+Math GPA to determine Co-op/Internship eligibility. This will count that course in your "Custom GPA".
"Save" will store all currently inputted data in your local storage.
WARNING: Clearing your browser's cookies and/or cache will ERASE your saved data.
"Load" will fill in the calcualtor using data previously stored locally.
WARNING: When loading previously saved data, current inputted data will be LOST.

NOTE: Correctness of results is not guaranteed if these instructions are not followed. Please double check your inputs when using the calculator.
